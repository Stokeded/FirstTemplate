Type: Multi-Mark Medium/Heavy Battlefield Transport Helicopter for UK MoD

The Boeing Chinook is a large, tandem rotor helicopter operated by the Royal Air Force (RAF). A series of variants based on the United States Army's Boeing CH-47 Chinook, the RAF Chinook fleet is the largest outside the United States Army.

  • First flight: 23 March 1980 (HC1)
  • Manufacturer: Boeing Defense, Space & Security
  • Number built: 72 + 16 ordered
